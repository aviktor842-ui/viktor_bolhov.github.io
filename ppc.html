PPC Campaign Plan

Example paid search setup for the “noise cancelling headphones” niche.

Platform, goal, targeting & budget

PPC platform: Google Ads (Search Campaign) + remarketing via Google Display Network.
Campaign goal: Drive high-intent users to the comparison landing page and generate “recommendation requests” via the contact form.
Target audience: 18–55, commuters, remote workers, frequent travelers. Locations: major cities + airports (optional). Devices: mobile + desktop.
Landing page: products.html (use-case comparison with CTA to Contact).

Primary keyword focus: noise cancelling headphones
Supporting keywords: best noise cancelling headphones, ANC headphones for travel, noise cancelling headphones for work calls, over ear noise cancelling headphones.

Example budget: €20/day for Search (≈ €600/month) + €5/day for remarketing (≈ €150/month). Bid strategy: Maximize Conversions with a target CPA once conversion volume is adequate.

Sample ad (responsive search ad)

Headlines (examples):

  • Best Noise Cancelling Headphones (2026)
  • Compare ANC for Travel, Work & Commute
  • Comfort + Strong ANC — Top Picks

Descriptions (examples):

  • Find the right ANC headphones fast. Compare comfort, battery, and call quality by use‑case.
  • Not sure what to buy? See our top picks and get a recommendation based on your routine.
